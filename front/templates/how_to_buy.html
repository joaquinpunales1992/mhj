How to buy a house in Japan

These are the steps to owning your house in Japan.

  1. Express your interest in any of the available properties.
  2. Have an initial conversation with our team.
  3. Sign the service contract and choose a certified real estate agent from our available options.
  4. Define the properties you'd like to inspect (personally or virtually).
  5. Formalize your offer for the property you've chosen.
  6. Finalize the details, costs, and documentation.
  7. Receive the keys to your new home in Japan.

Foreigners can buy property in Japan with no residency requirement and full ownership — you can complete the process remotely and visit whenever you choose.
